Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate?

The hackathon is open to all university students across India. As long as you are enrolled in a college program, you are welcome to join.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in Paranox 2.O is completely free of charge. We believe innovation should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need to be a coder to join?

Not at all! Teams need a mix of skills. Designers, project managers, and business-minded students are just as valuable as coders. A diverse team is a strong team.

What if I don't have a team?

Don't worry! You can reach out to me, and I can help connect you with other students from JIT Nashik who are looking for team members.

A Simple 5-Step Guide to Participate

1

Form Your A-Team

Assemble a team of 3 to 5 passionate innovators. Diversity in skills is your greatest strength!

2

Register Before the Deadline

Use the referral link on this page to fill in your team details and secure your spot. Don't wait until the last minute!

3

Brainstorm & Submit Your Idea

Once registered, work with your team to brainstorm a groundbreaking idea and submit it before the submission deadline.

4

Develop Your Prototype

If your idea is shortlisted, it's time to code, design, and build! Turn your vision into a functional prototype.

5

Compete in the Grand Finale

The best teams will be invited to the offline Grand Finale in Delhi NCR to pitch their projects to judges and win!
