About Paranox Contest 2.O

Welcome to Paranox Contest 2.O, a national innovation challenge by TechXNinjas aimed at fostering creativity, technical brilliance, and real-world problem-solving among college students across India. This year, we’re going bigger with prizes, internship opportunities, mentorship, and a dynamic competition structure. Get ready to take your ideas from code to concept, from prototype to pitch!

Eligibility & Rules

Who Can Participate?

  • ✓ Open to students from all Indian universities (UG & PG).
  • ✓ Age range: 17-25 years.
  • ✓ Teams must have 3 to 5 members.
  • ✓ Inter-college and cross-specialization teams are welcome.

Guidelines

  • ! The team leader must submit entries on behalf of the team.
  • ! Multiple entries from the same team will be disqualified.
  • ! Plagiarized submissions will be strictly disqualified.
  • ! The jury’s decisions will be final and binding.
